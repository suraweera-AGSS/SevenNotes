About Us

Overview

Welcome to SevenNotes.lk, your ultimate destination for exploring and enjoying the mesmerizing world of Sinhala music. We take pride in providing a platform that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka through its diverse and enchanting musical landscape.

Our Mission

At SevenNotes.lk, our mission is to make Sinhala music accessible to everyone, fostering a deep appreciation for the artistry, tradition, and emotion woven into each song. We strive to create a seamless and enjoyable experience for our users, offering a vast collection of songs along with features like lyrics, curated playlists, and more.
